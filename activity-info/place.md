# Location and address

Your physical business address and service coverage area.

_The address of your business is used by the AI assistant to give precise directions to customers calling to visit you or to request deliveries in your area._

## Search with Google Places <a id="ricerca-google-places"></a>

Start typing the address in the main field: the Google Places autocomplete suggests real addresses and auto-fills city, province, ZIP code and country. You can always edit individual fields manually if needed.

## Required fields <a id="campi-obbligatori"></a>

- Address (street, number)
- City
- Country

> [!TIP]
> **Location status**
> At the bottom of the card you'll see a green confirmation icon when the address is complete. If required fields are missing, a yellow warning appears: complete them to give the AI accurate answers.
