# Opening hours

Standard hours and special days (holidays, events).

_Opening hours are the source of truth the AI assistant uses to tell customers when you're open, accept bookings and correctly handle after-hours calls._

## Regular hours <a id="orari-regolari"></a>

For each day of the week you can choose: closed, open 24/7 or one or more time slots (e.g. 09:00-13:00 and 15:00-19:00). Slots let you handle the lunch break, double shifts or evening openings.

## Special hours <a id="orari-speciali"></a>

Add exception periods for holidays, summer breaks, extraordinary closures or special events. For each period specify start date, end date, reason (e.g. “Christmas”) and whether you're closed or open with different hours.

> [!TIP]
> **Plan ahead**
> Add holidays and time off well in advance: the assistant will warn customers calling during those periods and can suggest alternative dates for bookings or appointments.
