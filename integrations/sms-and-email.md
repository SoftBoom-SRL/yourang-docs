# SMS and email

Notifications and reminders via SMS and email

_SMS and email are the universal channels. Almost everyone has an email inbox and a phone. Yourang uses them to send automatic confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups._

## SMS and email <a id="cosa-sono-sms-email"></a>

- **SMS** — Short messages that reach the phone. Perfect for reminders and urgent matters
- **Email** — Longer, richer messages. Ideal for detailed confirmations and formal communications

## When they are sent <a id="quando-vengono-inviati"></a>

1. Right after the booking (confirmation)
2. The day before the appointment (reminder)
3. After the call (feedback and follow-up)

## Customizable sender <a id="mittente-personalizzato"></a>

You can choose the name that appears as sender (e.g. "Hotel Luna" for email, "LUNA" for SMS). Customers will see your business name, not Yourang.

## Reliable delivery <a id="deliverability"></a>

> [!TIP]
> **SMS: guaranteed delivery**
> SMS and email have very high delivery rates. If an SMS fails to arrive due to a wrong number, Yourang flags it so you can correct it.

For email, we recommend adding the Yourang address to your provider's whitelist to prevent messages from ending up in spam.
