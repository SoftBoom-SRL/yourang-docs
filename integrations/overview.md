# Integrations overview

What an integration means and which systems are supported

_Integrations connect Yourang to your favorite systems: calendar, WhatsApp, email, SMS, business software. Yourang communicates automatically and keeps everything in sync._

## What integrations mean <a id="cosa-significano-le-integrazioni"></a>

An integration is a connection between Yourang and an external service. Once connected, Yourang talks to that service automatically: it reads information, sends messages, updates appointments. Nothing manual.

For example: when a guest calls to book a room, Yourang checks your calendar to see which rooms are free, then books automatically and sends a confirmation by email.

## Supported systems <a id="sistemi-supportati"></a>

- [**Calendar**](#calendario) — Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Calendly
- [**WhatsApp**](#whatsapp) — Automatic messages and notifications
- [**SMS**](#sms-e-email) — Notifications and reminders via SMS
- [**Email**](#sms-e-email) — Confirmations, reminders, follow-ups
- [**Business software and PMS**](#gestionale) — Hotels, restaurants, data alignment
- [**Webhooks**](#webhook) — Notifications to your systems

## How it works <a id="come-funziona"></a>

- Choose the service you want to connect (e.g. Google Calendar)
- Log in to your account on that service
- Yourang is authorized to access the data
- Synchronization starts automatically

> [!TIP]
> **No code required**
> All integrations are configured from the Yourang dashboard. No technical skills required.
