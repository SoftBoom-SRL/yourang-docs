# Business software and PMS

Sync data with your management system

_If you use a management system (hotel, restaurant, reservations), you can connect it to Yourang. The voice assistant will always have up-to-date data and will be able to book directly into your system._

## Which management systems are supported <a id="quali-gestionali"></a>

- Horus (hotel)
- Opera (hotel)
- Ical (hotel)
- Table Management (restaurants)
- Reservation Manager (generic reservations)
- Systems with REST API (if properly implemented)

## What Yourang syncs <a id="cosa-sincronizza-yourang"></a>

- Availability (free rooms, available tables, hours)
- Rates and prices
- Available services (breakfast, wifi, parking)
- Reservations (Yourang adds them in real time)

## If you don't have a management system <a id="se-non-hai-gestionale"></a>

> [!TIP]
> **Nothing to worry about**
> If you do not use a management system, Yourang still works. You can manage reservations manually via the calendar or import them from Excel. Yourang will always have the data you enter.

In this case, we recommend using Google Calendar + WhatsApp/Email for a practical and automated flow.

## Real-time synchronization <a id="sincronizzazione-bidirezionale"></a>

Yourang continuously syncs with your management system. If you cancel a reservation in your system, Yourang knows within seconds. If a guest books via Yourang, it appears immediately in your management system.
