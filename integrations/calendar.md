# Calendar

Sync your calendar with Yourang

_Connect your calendar to Yourang and the voice assistant will always have up-to-date schedules. Two-way sync: Yourang reads the calendar and can add appointments automatically._

## Which calendars are supported <a id="quali-calendari-sono-supportati"></a>

- Google Calendar
- Apple Calendar (iCloud)
- Calendly
- Outlook

## What you can do <a id="cosa-puoi-fare"></a>

- **Check availability** — Yourang reads the calendar and knows when the next free slot is
- **Book automatically** — The guest books directly during the call. Yourang adds the event to your calendar
- **Avoid conflicts** — If the calendar is full, Yourang communicates this to the customer
- **Automatic reminders** — Yourang can send a message to the customer the day before the reservation

## How to connect the calendar <a id="come-collegare-il-calendario"></a>

1. **Access the Yourang dashboard** — Go to Integrations > Calendar
2. **Choose your provider** — Click on Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Calendly, or Outlook
3. **Authorize Yourang** — Log in with your account. Yourang will ask for permission to read and modify events
4. **Choose the target calendar** — Select which calendar Yourang should sync (e.g. "Reservations")

## Privacy and data <a id="privacy-e-dati"></a>

> [!NOTE]
> **Your data stays yours**
> Yourang syncs only the necessary information (dates, times, event titles). Nothing is stored on our servers beyond what is needed. You can revoke access at any time from the settings.
