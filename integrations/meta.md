# Meta (Instagram and Facebook)

Connect Instagram and Facebook to reply to messages and comments

_The Meta integration connects your Facebook Page and professional Instagram account to Yourang. From that point an AI agent can automatically reply to direct messages and moderate comments._

## How it works <a id="come-funziona-meta"></a>

From the Integrations section press Connect on the Meta card, sign in with the Facebook profile that administers the Page, and authorize Yourang to manage messages and comments. The connection is done once and enables both Instagram and Facebook together.

## What you can do <a id="cosa-puoi-fare-con-meta"></a>

- Automatically reply to Instagram DMs
- Reply to messages on your Facebook Page
- Reply to comments on posts
- Hide abusive or spam comments
- Follow all conversations from a unified inbox

> [!NOTE]
> **Professional Instagram account**
> For Instagram messages and comments, the account must be Professional (Business or Creator) and linked to a Facebook Page you administer.

> [!TIP]
> **Learn more in the Meta Agent section**
> Agent configuration, DM reply modes, comment moderation, and the inbox are all described in the Meta Agent section.
