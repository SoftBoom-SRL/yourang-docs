# WhatsApp

Communicate with customers via WhatsApp

_WhatsApp is the preferred channel for customers to receive confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups. Yourang sends automatic messages without you doing anything._

## How it works <a id="come-funziona-whatsapp"></a>

Yourang connects to WhatsApp Business via a dedicated number (not your personal number). From this number, the assistant sends messages to your customers according to the rules you configure.

## What you can do <a id="cosa-puoi-fare-con-whatsapp"></a>

- Send booking confirmations
- Send reminders the day before
- Ask for feedback after a reservation
- Answer frequently asked questions
- Send updates (e.g. seasonal menus, offers)

## The dedicated number <a id="numero-dedicato"></a>

> [!WARNING]
> **WhatsApp Business number**
> Yourang uses a dedicated number, not your personal mobile. This number is registered to your business and appears as "Business" in your customers' contacts. You will not be able to use it for personal messages.

## Message templates <a id="template-messaggi"></a>

Yourang comes with standard baseline templates (e.g. "Thank you for your booking"). You can customize the text from the settings to add your company tone.

> [!TIP]
> **Personalization**
> Add the name of your hotel or restaurant in messages to make communication more personal.
