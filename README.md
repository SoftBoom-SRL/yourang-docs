# Everything you need to use the platform

> Mirrored from the yourang monorepo. **Do not edit files here directly** — changes are overwritten by the export script.

A guide designed for business owners who want to understand, step by step, how to hand off calls to a voice assistant.

## Featured sections

- **Get started** — Understand what the platform does, create your account, and activate your first call.
- **Dashboard Assistant** — The AI built into the dashboard: it helps you configure, read your data and move between pages.
- **Activity information** — Configure your business name, address, opening hours, and departments.
- **Voice agent** — Choose voice, language, and instructions: give your assistant a real identity.
- **Models offered** — Available AI voices and models: how they work and how to pick the right combination.
- **Documents and knowledge base** — Upload documents and procedures: the assistant consults them to reply with accurate information.
- **Calls** — History, transcripts, summaries, recordings, and export.
- **Call transfers** — Departments, system tools, after-hours handling, and routing rules.
- **WhatsApp** — Chats, automations, approved templates, contacts, and targeted campaigns.
- **Meta Agent** — Automatic replies to Instagram and Facebook DMs and comment moderation.
- **Actions and campaigns** — SMS and email campaigns and automated sequences with segmentation and tracking.
- **Reservations** — Unified calendar, availability rules, confirmations, and changes.
- **Contacts** — Customer directory, CSV import, lists, and custom fields.
- **Shop and catalogue** — Product and service catalogue, orders, OCR, and price-list import.
- **Integrations** — Calendar, WhatsApp, SMS, email, business systems, and webhooks.
- **Workflows** — Orchestrate actions, decisions, and waits in multi-step visual automations.
- **Call center and dialer** — Outbound campaigns, human operators in conference with the AI, and real-time monitoring.
- **External API and developers** — REST API and webhooks to integrate Yourang with business systems, PMS, and custom automations.
- **Use cases** — Concrete examples for hotels, restaurants, B&Bs, beauty centres, and travel agencies.
- **Pricing and plans** — Subscription plans, wallet, credits, usage, and billing.
- **Management** — Account, billing, team, security, and notifications.
- **Resources** — FAQs, glossary, what's new, and support.
