# Restaurant

Table bookings, menu information, and handling special requests without tying up the floor staff

_In a restaurant, the phone rings often at the least convenient moments: during service, at lunch or dinner peaks. Yourang always answers, handling bookings and information requests without distracting the floor staff._

## Main scenarios <a id="scenari-principali"></a>

- **Table booking** — The customer indicates date, time, and number of covers. Yourang checks availability, records the booking, and sends a confirmation. If the requested time is not available, it proposes alternatives.
- **Change or cancellation** — The customer who wants to move or cancel a booking is guided by the assistant through the process, updating the system automatically.
- **Menu information** — Yourang answers questions about specials of the day, fixed menu, vegetarian or vegan options, and specific ingredients in case of allergies or intolerances.
- **Special requests** — High chair for children, reserved table for a special occasion, birthday cake: the assistant collects the requests and notes them in the booking.
- **Practical information** — Opening hours, address, nearby parking, accessibility for the disabled: frequent questions that Yourang answers without engaging staff.

> [!NOTE]
> **Update the menu in the knowledge base**
> To ensure Yourang answers correctly about available dishes, remember to update the knowledge base whenever the menu changes, including seasonally.

## Handling traffic peaks <a id="gestione-picchi-di-traffico"></a>

During peak times - Friday and Saturday evenings, holidays, high season periods - call volume increases significantly. Yourang handles multiple calls simultaneously without putting anyone on hold, guaranteeing the same quality of response regardless of inflow.

> [!TIP]
> **Automatic waiting list**
> If the restaurant is full, you can configure Yourang to put the customer on a waiting list and contact them automatically in case of cancellation.
