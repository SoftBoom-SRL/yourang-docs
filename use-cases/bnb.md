# B&Bs and short-term rentals

An always-on virtual reception for those managing properties without permanent staff

_Running a B&B or a short-term rental apartment often means being reachable at any hour, even at the most inconvenient times. Yourang becomes your virtual reception: it answers calls, provides information to guests, and handles routine communications, so you can dedicate your time to the activities that truly matter._

## 24/7 virtual reception <a id="reception-virtuale-247"></a>

Unlike a structured hotel, those who run a B&B or short-term rental rarely can afford staff dedicated to communications. Yourang bridges this gap, guaranteeing a professional response to every call, day and night.

## Main benefits <a id="vantaggi-principali"></a>

- **Self check-in** — Yourang provides guests with access instructions (key safe code, gate code, key location) when they need them, without requiring your physical presence.
- **Property information** — The assistant answers questions about Wi-Fi, parking, house rules, check-out times, and any other information you would normally have sent by message.
- **No missed calls** — Even when you are busy or unavailable, potential guests always receive a response. This reduces lost bookings due to missed calls.
- **First-level emergency handling** — If a guest has a problem (heating, water, electricity), Yourang collects the information and sends you a notification with a summary of the situation.
- **Professional response** — The assistant introduces itself with the name of your property and maintains a professional, consistent tone, improving the perception of the service.

> [!TIP]
> **Update instructions before each arrival**
> If you use temporary access codes that change for each guest, you can update the information in the knowledge base directly from the dashboard, in seconds.

## When it alerts you directly <a id="quando-ti-avvisa"></a>

Yourang contacts you - via notification, SMS, or email - in situations that require your direct attention: complex booking requests, complaints, emergencies, or questions for which it does not have an answer in the knowledge base.
