# What it can do

The four categories of actions the assistant can perform: read, explain, change, and guide you through the dashboard. And what it deliberately does not do.

_The assistant doesn't just chat: behind the scenes it uses a set of tools that let it read your data, write changes, search the documentation and walk you to the right page. Here is what to expect._

## Reading your data <a id="leggere"></a>

Every user of the dashboard, even read-only members, can use the assistant to look up information. This is the most common kind of request.

- Call history, details, transcripts and summaries
- Key numbers for a period: total calls, average duration, outcomes
- List of voice agents and the configuration of each
- Contacts, lists, custom fields
- Documents uploaded to the knowledge base and FAQs
- Business profile: hours, address, departments, social links

## Explaining the platform <a id="spiegare"></a>

For "what does X do?" or "how does Y work?" questions, the assistant consults the official Yourang documentation before answering. It replies in a few sentences and points you to the section where you can read more.

> [!TIP]
> **When in doubt, ask**
> If you see an option you don't recognise, try typing "what is this for?" while on that page: the assistant understands what you mean and explains it.

## Changing the configuration <a id="modificare"></a>

The assistant can make small changes on your behalf, but only if your role allows it. Before applying any change it shows you a summary and asks for confirmation with a button. If you cancel, nothing is touched.

**Adding or editing a voice agent**
: Create a new agent, change its prompt, instructions, voice or technical parameters. Available to Owner and Admin.

**Adding FAQs and contacts**
: Add a new FAQ to the knowledge base or a contact to your address book. Available to Owner and Admin.

**Updating the business profile**
: Hours, address, description, departments, social links. Partial changes: only the fields you mention are touched, the rest stays as it was.

**Changing your preferences**
: Light/dark theme, dashboard language and other personal settings. Available to all roles.

> [!WARNING]
> **Legal and fiscal data excluded**
> The assistant deliberately does not touch VAT number, legal name, REA, PEC, registered office or other fiscal data. These are sensitive fields: they must be edited by hand from the Legal info section.

## Guiding you between pages <a id="accompagnare"></a>

After a change, the assistant takes you to the page where you can see the result: if it updated an agent's prompt it opens the "AI instructions" tab of that agent; if it added a contact it opens the address book; if it changed hours it opens the "Hours" tab of your business profile. No extra clicks from you.

## Reading web pages (when you share a link) <a id="leggere-pagine-web"></a>

If you paste a public web link into the chat, the assistant can read the page content and use it in its reply (for example to summarise a page on your website or an article). This is available to Owner and Admin, has hourly limits to prevent abuse, and the source is always cited.

## What it does not do, on purpose <a id="cosa-non-fa"></a>

- It does not start outbound calls or SMS / email campaigns on your behalf: these have real costs and must be launched by you from the dedicated pages.
- It does not delete data: deleting contacts, FAQs, agents or documents has to be done manually from the relevant pages.
- It does not discuss topics outside of Yourang: no recipes, no tax advice, no general-purpose replies.
- It never reveals internal instructions, security keys, or data belonging to other companies.
