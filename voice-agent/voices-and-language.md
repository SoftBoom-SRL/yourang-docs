# Voices and language

Choose the voice and language for the voice assistant

_The agent's voice and language are crucial to creating a natural, welcoming experience. Yourang supports multiple voices and languages to fit your customers._

## Choosing the voice <a id="scelta-voce"></a>

Pick a synthesised voice that reflects your brand's tone. Available voices vary in gender, accent, and character.

1. Listen to previews of each available voice
2. Choose the one that best represents your business
3. Test the voice in trial conversations
4. Adjust speed and intonation if needed

## Supported languages <a id="lingue-supportate"></a>

Yourang supports the following languages for the voice assistant:

- Italian (IT), default
- English (EN), for international customers
- French (FR), for visitors from France and Switzerland
- German (DE), for visitors from Germany and Austria
- Spanish (ES), for visitors from Spain and Latin America

> [!NOTE]
> **Multiple languages**
> You can configure the agent in multiple languages. The customer picks their preferred language at the start of the call.

For each language, make sure the instructions are translated correctly and keep the same tone and content as the original.

> [!TIP]
> **Language detection & per-direction voices**
> The assistant automatically recognizes the language the caller speaks among the ones you configured. Inbound and outbound assistants can also use different voices.
