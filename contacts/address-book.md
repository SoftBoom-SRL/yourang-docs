# Customer directory

Your centralised archive of customers and guests

_The customer directory is the centralised archive of everyone who has interacted with your business through Yourang. Each entry automatically gathers the information that surfaced during calls, giving you a complete view of every customer without entering anything manually._

## What is saved automatically <a id="cosa-viene-salvato"></a>

At the end of every call handled by the voice assistant, Yourang updates the customer profile with the relevant information that emerged from the conversation. No manual work is required: the system recognises the caller's number and matches the data to the corresponding profile.

- **First and last name** — Detected from the customer's introduction during the call or entered manually.
- **Phone number** — Automatically recorded from the caller's number.
- **Date and time of last interaction** — Updated with every new call, so you always know when you last heard from the customer.
- **Call summary** — A short AI-generated summary with the main points of the conversation.

> [!TIP]
> **Returning customers**
> When a returning customer calls, the assistant can recognise them and personalise the greeting or recall previous information, making the experience more welcoming.

## The customer profile <a id="profilo-cliente"></a>

Every contact in the directory has a profile page with all the information collected over time. From here you can review the interaction history, edit the data, and add personal notes.

**Name**
: First and last name of the customer, editable at any time.

**Phone**
: Number used for calls. It can be associated with multiple contacts only if it's a shared line.

**Email**
: Email address, useful for sending confirmations or written communications.

**Preferred language**
: The language the customer prefers to be contacted in. The voice assistant adapts automatically.

**Notes**
: Free field for internal annotations: preferences, special needs, information useful for the staff.

**Recent calls**
: List of the most recent interactions with date, duration, and summary.

**Linked bookings**
: Any history of bookings made through the assistant.

## How to view a profile <a id="come-consultare"></a>

1. **Open the Contacts section** — From the sidebar select "Contacts" to access the full directory.
2. **Search for the customer** — Use the search bar to find a customer by first name, last name, or phone number.
3. **Open the profile** — Click the customer's name to view the full profile with all information and history.

> [!NOTE]
> **Privacy and data handling**
> All customer data is processed in compliance with the GDPR. Make sure you have informed your customers about the processing of personal data in line with current regulations.
