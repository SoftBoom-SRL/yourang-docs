# Consumption and invoices

Monitor used minutes, view invoices, and keep monthly trends under control.

_Understanding how your property is using Yourang is the first step to optimizing cost. The panel provides clear tools to see aggregate consumption, per-call details, and all issued invoices._

## The Consumption page <a id="vista-consumo"></a>

In the Plan management -> Consumption section you'll find a chart showing minutes consumed day by day, compared with the quota included in the plan. A progress bar tells you at a glance how much you've already used and how much remains before starting to draw from the wallet.

- **Minutes included in the plan** — How many minutes you are entitled to use in this billing cycle.
- **Minutes used** — How many minutes you have actually used so far in the current cycle, divided by type (incoming, outgoing, transfers).
- **Remaining minutes** — How many minutes remain before starting to deduct from the wallet. If already zero, you'll see how much of the wallet has been consumed.
- **Next renewal date** — When the counter resets and the new cycle begins.

## Payment history and invoices <a id="cronologia-pagamenti"></a>

The Payment history page lists all transactions: subscription renewals and wallet top-ups. For each entry you see date, amount, payment method, and outcome. Clicking on an entry lets you download the invoice in PDF.

> [!NOTE]
> **Invoices and accounting**
> Invoices are issued with the legal data registered in the Legal information section. Verify that company name, address, and VAT number are correct before the first charge: once issued, the invoice can only be modified through a credit note.

## Exporting data for accounting <a id="esportare-i-dati"></a>

For accounting reconciliation or internal analysis you can export to CSV both the payment history and the wallet movements, filtering by date range. The file includes all relevant fields: date, description, amount, VAT, invoice reference.

## Anomalous consumption alerts <a id="avvisi-su-consumo-anomalo"></a>

Yourang alerts you via email when consumption grows significantly compared to the average or when you are about to exceed the quota included in the plan. These alerts let you react in time: upgrade the plan, enable auto-topup, or investigate any anomalous use.
