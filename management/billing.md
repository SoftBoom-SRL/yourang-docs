# Billing and subscription

Manage your plan and invoices

_Billing management is simple and transparent. Here you can view your current plan, monitor consumption, access invoices, and update the payment method._

## Your plan <a id="il-tuo-piano"></a>

The current plan is displayed at the top of the billing section. It clearly shows which features are available and what usage limits apply to your subscription.

Yourang offers flexible plans suited to different needs. Each plan includes a certain number of call minutes, manageable contacts, and advanced features. If your consumption grows, you can always upgrade to a higher plan directly from the platform.

## Consumption and usage <a id="consumo-utilizzo"></a>

An intuitive dashboard shows your current consumption in the current billing cycle. You can see how many call minutes you have used, how many contacts you are managing, and which premium features you are using.

> [!NOTE]
> **Real-time monitoring**
> Consumption is updated in real time. If you are about to exceed a limit, you will receive a timely notification so you can make informed decisions.

## Invoices <a id="fatture"></a>

Access all your monthly invoices in a single section. Each invoice is numbered, dated, and contains the complete breakdown of charges, VAT, and the total amount due.

Invoices are issued automatically at the end of each billing cycle and sent by email to the registered contact address. You can download them in PDF for your accounting records.

## Payment method <a id="metodo-di-pagamento"></a>

Yourang accepts the main credit and debit cards. Your payment method is used for automatic charging at the end of each billing period.

You can update the payment method at any time. If the payment fails for technical reasons, you will receive a notification and can resolve the issue before your subscription is suspended.
