# Team and roles

Invite your colleagues and manage permissions

_Yourang is designed to be used by multiple people in your organization. Here you can invite colleagues, assign them specific roles, and manage their access permissions._

## Roles and permissions <a id="ruoli-e-permessi"></a>

Each team member has a role that defines which actions they can perform in Yourang. Roles are structured hierarchically, from most restrictive to most permissive.

**Owner**
: Has total control of the account. Can modify organization data, manage the team, access billing, and modify security settings. Should be assigned only to the holder of legal responsibility.

**Administrator**
: Has almost all the Owner's permissions, except billing management and account deletion. Ideal for the internal Yourang manager.

**Member**
: Limited access to operational features. Can view contacts, campaigns, manage operators, and monitor calls, but cannot modify critical organization settings.

## Inviting colleagues to the team <a id="invitare-colleghi"></a>

An invitation is the first step to adding a new member to your team. The process is fast and intuitive.

1. **Go to the Team section** — Access the organization settings and select the Team and roles tab.
2. **Click on "Invite member"** — A form will ask you to enter the email address of the person you wish to invite.
3. **Select the role** — Choose which role you wish to assign to the new member. Remember that you can always change it later.
4. **Send the invitation** — The person will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation and complete registration. The invitation remains valid for 7 days.

## Managing the team <a id="gestire-il-team"></a>

In the list of team members you can view the status of each person, the assigned role, and the date of last access. From here you can modify roles, temporarily deactivate a member, or remove them completely.

> [!TIP]
> **Role management**
> If a colleague changes responsibilities, you can update their role at any time without losing data or access to the projects they are working on.
