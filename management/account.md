# Account and organization

Manage your business data

_Your organization data is the heart of Yourang configuration. Here you can update your business information, opening hours, and contact details that customers will see when interacting with the voice assistant._

## Business data <a id="dati-attivita"></a>

The section dedicated to business data contains all the identifying information for your organization. This data is used in various parts of Yourang and contributes to giving your voice assistant a consistent identity.

- **Company name** — The official name of your business, as it appears in legal documents and formal communications.
- **Address** — The physical address of your business, important for customers seeking directions or wishing to visit in person.
- **Phone and email** — The main contacts through which customers can reach you. Make sure they are always up to date and monitored.
- **Opening hours** — Your business operating hours for each day of the week. The voice assistant will use these hours to provide accurate information.

## Tax data <a id="dati-fiscali"></a>

Tax data is necessary for invoice issuance and proper administrative management of your subscription.

> [!WARNING]
> **Attention to tax data**
> Tax data is strictly confidential and is used only for billing. Make sure the VAT number and tax code are correct before confirming, as this data will appear on all issued invoices. Subsequent changes may require manual handling.

- VAT number
- Tax code
- Fiscal address (if different from the head office)
- Name of the tax representative
