# WhatsApp AI agents

Create AI agents that handle WhatsApp conversations automatically.

_AI agents bring the same conversational intelligence as the voice assistant to WhatsApp: they understand messages in natural language, reply in your business's tone, and hand over to your team when a person is needed._

## What an AI agent can do <a id="cosa-fanno"></a>

- **Instant replies** — The agent answers common questions immediately, at any time of day, without keeping the customer waiting.
- **Business knowledge** — It draws on your business information to give accurate answers about hours, services, and availability.
- **Handover to the team** — When the request needs a person, the conversation passes to your team with the full context already in the chat.

## Where to find it <a id="dove-si-trova"></a>

Open WhatsApp → AI Agents from the side menu to create and manage your agents.

> [!NOTE]
> **Agents and automations work together**
> Automations handle fixed rules (after-hours replies, routing); AI agents handle free-form conversations. An active WhatsApp number is required.
