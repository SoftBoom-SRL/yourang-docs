# WhatsApp Business in Yourang

What you can do with WhatsApp and why it's a natural complement to the voice assistant.

_WhatsApp is today the channel customers prefer for quick requests, confirmations, and informal bookings. Yourang gives you the tools to manage it professionally, integrated with the rest of the platform._

## Why WhatsApp <a id="vantaggi"></a>

- **Convenience for the customer** — Many people prefer to write a message rather than call. WhatsApp lets you capture these requests without forcing the customer to change channel.
- **Asynchrony** — Unlike a call, a WhatsApp conversation can move at the customer's pace: your team responds when they can.
- **Traceability** — Everything stays in writing: no ambiguity about what was said, when, or by whom. Particularly useful for complex requests.
- **Continuity with the call** — A conversation started on the phone can continue on WhatsApp (to send a link, a confirmation, a map) and vice versa.

## The WhatsApp sections of the panel <a id="componenti"></a>

- **Chat** — The interface where your team manages conversations in real time.
- **Automations** — Automatic replies for typical messages, after-hours, or to route the conversation.
- **Templates** — Message templates approved by WhatsApp, required to write to customers who haven't written first.
- **Contacts and lists** — Dedicated WhatsApp address book, segmentation for targeted campaigns.
- **AI agents** — AI agents that reply to WhatsApp conversations automatically and hand over to the team when needed.

> [!NOTE]
> **You need a number and Meta approval**
> To activate WhatsApp Business in Yourang you must have a dedicated number (not already used on consumer WhatsApp) and complete the Meta approval process. The support team guides the initial setup.
