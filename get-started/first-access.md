# First access

How to create your account, set up your business, and activate the first call handled by Yourang.

_From account creation to activating your first call, only a few steps are needed. This page walks you through the entire process from start to finish, without skipping anything._

## Create your account <a id="crea-il-tuo-account"></a>

1. **Receive the invite or sign up** — If your business already has a subscription, you'll receive an invitation email from your manager. Click the link in the email and choose a secure password. If you're starting from scratch, visit the sign-up page and enter your business details.
2. **Verify your email address** — After registering you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the button inside it to activate your account. Check your spam folder too if you don't see it within a few minutes.
3. **Enter your business details** — On first access you'll be asked to complete your organisation profile: business name, address, and main phone number. These details appear in the panel header and are used by the voice assistant to introduce itself to customers.
4. **Set up your first agent** — An agent is the voice assistant assigned to a phone number. Give it a name, choose the response language, and set the initial greeting customers will hear when they call. You can customise it further at any time.
5. **Upload information about your business** — Provide the agent with everything it needs to respond correctly: opening hours, menu or price list, cancellation policies, frequently asked questions. You can upload documents in PDF format or paste the text directly into the panel.
6. **Connect your phone number** — Link your business phone number to the agent. The Yourang team will guide you through this technical step: in most cases it's a simple configuration that requires no changes to your existing setup.
7. **Make a test call** — Before going live, call the number from your personal phone to hear how the assistant sounds. Verify the greeting, ask a few typical customer questions, and make sure the answers are correct. If something is off, edit the information you uploaded and run the test again.

> [!NOTE]
> **Your existing number stays yours**
> You won't have to change the phone number your customers already know. Yourang integrates with your current number, with no migrations or service interruptions.

## What to do right after <a id="cosa-fare-subito-dopo"></a>

- Read the Platform Overview section to learn where to find every feature.
- Invite the collaborators who need access to the panel: you can assign different roles to each.
- Turn on email or in-app notifications so you're alerted about relevant calls.
- Explore the History section to see how transcripts and summaries are displayed.

> [!TIP]
> **Support during setup**
> If you have questions at any step, the Yourang support team is reachable via chat from the control panel. Businesses on the professional plan get guided onboarding with a dedicated consultant.
