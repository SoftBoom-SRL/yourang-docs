# What the platform is

Find out why Yourang exists and what problem it solves for your business.

_Yourang is the AI-powered voice assistance platform built for hotels, restaurants, bed & breakfasts, and travel agencies. It answers the phone for you, converses with customers naturally, and tracks every interaction, twenty-four hours a day, with no exceptions._

## The problem Yourang solves <a id="il-problema-che-yourang-risolve"></a>

Every day, accommodation properties and restaurants lose bookings, requests for information, and business opportunities because the phone rings and no one can pick up: peak hour in the kitchen, simultaneous check-ins, the day they're closed. Yourang eliminates this problem by installing an always-available voice assistant that answers, informs and, where needed, involves human staff at the right moment.

## What Yourang does <a id="cosa-fa-yourang"></a>

- [**Answers calls**](#) — The voice assistant handles incoming calls with a personalised greeting and a fluent conversation that matches the tone of your business.
- [**Manages bookings and requests**](#) — It checks availability, collects customer details, and shares hours, prices, and services without you lifting a finger.
- [**Transcribes and summarises**](#) — Every call is fully transcribed and condensed into a few key points, so you can review what matters in seconds.
- [**Transfers to an operator**](#) — When the situation calls for it, the assistant hands the call off to a human operator without the customer feeling any disruption.
- [**Learns about your business**](#) — Upload menus, price lists, procedures, and policies: the assistant studies them and replies precisely, like an experienced team member.
- [**Respects privacy**](#) — Call data is handled in compliance with current regulations. You decide what information to keep and for how long.

## How it works, in short <a id="come-funziona-in-breve"></a>

1. **The customer calls your number** — Your business phone number is configured on Yourang. When a call comes in, the assistant answers immediately with a personalised greeting.
2. **The assistant talks naturally** — The assistant asks questions, responds to requests, and manages the conversation by drawing on the information you've provided: menus, hours, rates, procedures.
3. **The call is recorded and transcribed** — When the conversation ends, Yourang produces a full transcript and a structured summary, immediately available in the control panel.
4. **You get notified when it matters** — If the assistant identifies a request that needs human attention, a complex booking, a complaint, an unusual situation, you can be alerted in real time.

> [!TIP]
> **No technical skills required**
> Yourang is built for people who run a business, not for IT specialists. Setup is guided step by step, and support is always available to help you.

Yourang doesn't replace your staff: it empowers them, freeing them from routine calls so they can focus on in-person hospitality and higher-value activities.
